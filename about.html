About BridgeX

We are a Southeast Asia-focused research and advisory firm committed to delivering rigorous, actionable insights.

Our Mission

Connecting Organizations
to Reliable Intelligence

BridgeX Cooperation was founded with a clear purpose: to bridge the gap between complex market realities and the strategic decisions organizations need to make. We believe that good decisions start with good data.

Operating across Southeast Asia, we combine internationally recognized research methodologies with deep local expertise — giving our clients the most accurate, culturally nuanced insights available.

Our Story

12 Years of Building
Research Bridges

2013

Founded in Vientiane

BridgeX Cooperation established with a focus on social research and field surveys for international development organizations.

2016

Regional Expansion

Extended operations into Vietnam and Cambodia, establishing field networks and local specialist partnerships.

2019

Digital Data Innovation

Adopted full digital data collection (CAPI/ODK) across all field operations, improving data quality and turnaround time by 40%.

2022

Expanded into commercial market research and feasibility studies, serving private sector clients across the region.
